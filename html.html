Fight Club (1999)

A story about a disillusioned man who forms an underground fight club.

Movie Overview

Fight Club, directed by David Fincher, is a cult film about the dark underbelly of consumerism and the human desire for rebellion. The film stars Brad Pitt, Edward Norton, and Helena Bonham Carter.
